Our Solution


mRNA vaccine pills which administer aptaswitch technology that prevents allergic reactions from taking place by binding to IgE

Preventative Solution

With our solution, we tackle the root cause of allergies, IgE, by preventing it from binding with enzymes within the body and triggering symptoms.

Easy-To-Use

We offer our product through oral administration which you can find at your local pharmacy. Easy for patients easy for pharmacies.

High Drug Lifetime

AllergX is effective for up to 2-3 weeks meaning compared to other daily treatments like antihistamines you only need take it 25 times a year.

Fast & Reliable

AllergX shows effects in less than 12 hours as opposed to alternate treatments. This is all achieved with almost no side effects.

Low Cost

A reduced frequency of use of our product as a result of its long lasting effects means lower allergy medication expenditure.

Pioneering Research

Our solution can be adapted to target many proteins beyond IgE, potentially offering a versatile platform for treating other diseases.
