Problem

Millions of people worldwide suffer from the life-threatening and debilitating effects of allergies. Current treatments range in effectiveness from life threatening to completely ineffective.

Solution

An mRNA Vaccine Pills which uses gene editing to provide effective treatment for allergies through the use of aptaswitch technology which binds to Immunoglobin E (IgE) and prevents allergic reactions.

Outcome

  • Improved quality of life: live your life how you want to live it
  • Decreased healthcare costs: reduce costly hospital/clinic visits
  • Innovation in allergy research: understanding the immune system

    Biotech Solution

    We use small molecules called aptamers, which bind specifically to IgE antibodies that cause allergic reactions. By preventing IgE from binding to allergens, Aptaswitch eliminates the risk of triggering an allergic reaction.

    Simple & Convenient

    Treatment is administered in the form of an mRNA Vaccine Pill that the user can take every 2-3 weeks. With low risk of side effects and simple technology, we make it easy for you to live life the way you want to.

    Long Lasting

    Our treatment is effective for up to 2-3 weeks. Overtime, the your body has the potential to adapt to release less IgE when encountering an allergen, resulting in a decreased severity of reactions outside of use of the pill, as well as potential loss of sensitivity to allergens.
